## Ingredients

- 32 oz Rye
- 20 oz Biscute
- 16 oz Caramel
- 10 oz Honey
- 3 # Breiss Amber DME
- 2 oz US Fuggle (pellets)
- 6 Gallons Water
- 1 Worfloc Tablet
- 12 oz unsulphered Molases

Heat 1.5 to 170, add grain, mash for 45 mins.  Heat 2.5 to 160, sparge grain, combine, chill 2 gallons
